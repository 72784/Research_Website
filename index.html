About the Group

The research group led by Dr. Mohammed E. Eltayeb focuses on high-frequency wireless communication systems, signal propagation, LiDAR-driven modeling, machine-learning assisted beam alignment, and intelligent wireless environments.

Research Focus Areas

mmWave & THz Communication

High-frequency signal propagation, beam management, NLoS modeling, and digital twins.

LiDAR-Aided Sensing

3D sensing for environment mapping, surface prediction, and scatter characterization.

Machine Learning for Wireless

AI-driven beam prediction, signal intelligence, and environment-awareness for 6G.
